Who We Are

A growing Indian construction & skilling company

VD Brother Projects Private Limited is a private limited company registered in India, founded with a simple mission — build dependable infrastructure and create real opportunities for the country's youth.

Headquartered in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, our team executes projects across four practice areas: gas pipeline works, home construction, commercial construction, and fees-based skill-development training. Every engagement is led by experienced engineers and supported by carefully vetted partners on the ground.

  • Registered Pvt Ltd company
  • GST & statutory compliant
  • Trained, in-house workforce
  • Modern equipment & tools
