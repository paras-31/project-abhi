Training & Certification

Industry-aligned training for India's youth

Our skill-development division runs fees-based, short-term training programs designed in collaboration with our own project teams. Trainees learn from real engineers and supervisors, using real-site tools and processes — not just textbooks.

Every program ends with a practical assessment and a certificate of completion, plus interview opportunities for our top performers.

  • Practical, on-tools training by working professionals
  • Small batch sizes for personal attention
  • Flexible weekday & weekend schedules
  • Certification on successful completion
  • Placement assistance for top trainees
  • Affordable, transparent fee structure
