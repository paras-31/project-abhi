About VD Brother

A trusted name in construction & skill development

VD Brother Projects Private Limited is a growing Indian infrastructure company specialising in gas-pipeline works, residential and commercial construction, and government-recognised skill-development programs. Headquartered in Rishikesh (Uttarakhand), we combine engineering excellence with grassroots execution.

Led by founders Mr. Dharmender Yadav and Mr. Virendra Kumar Yadav, our team delivers projects that are safe, sustainable and built to last.
