Reliable gas pipeline construction & commissioning

VD Brother Projects executes gas pipeline works for city gas distribution (CGD), industrial gas supply and last-mile residential connectivity. Our crews are trained in safe trenching, welding, pressure testing and commissioning — following Indian regulatory and PNGRB-aligned best practices.

  • Route survey, marking & right-of-way coordination
  • Excavation, trenching & HDD where required
  • Steel & MDPE pipe welding by certified welders
  • Hydro & pneumatic pressure testing
  • Cathodic protection & coating works
  • Commissioning, maintenance & emergency response
